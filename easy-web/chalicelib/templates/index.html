Get Started With Easy Exhaust

We've got what you need!

The application will not keep any GPS data, or personally identifiable information and will also not have any relevant access to the users Steemit or Strava account.

Users will need to have a Steemit and also be using the Exhaust(https://xhaust.me/) application to log activities.

Users will log their activities to Strava as they normally will, but if they also want to have the activity posted to Steemit, they will need to tag the title of their activity with the EXST tag, for example; "EXST - Morning Run".

The details of the activity will be taken from Strava and posted for them including any descriptions of training and hopefully images posted to Strava.
